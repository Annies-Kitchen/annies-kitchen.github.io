About us

A taste of 马来西亚
(Malaysia)

Annie's Kitchen is a small, home food business based in Wichita, Kansas. We have been a popular takeout spot for many locals (especially hungry WSU students) for years! Annie specializes in Malaysian-Chinese cuisine. The dish available varies daily and is posted on Facebook.
